To use IMIGO.ME you need to associate an email address with your VK account. Use this VK page to do so or navigate manually following the screenshots below.

Go to your VK Settings.

Set your new primary email address. After that you can try to login again.

To use IMIGO.ME you need to associate an email address with your Facebook account. Use this Facebook page to do so or navigate manually following the screenshots below.

Go to your Facebook Settings.

Go to General and set your new primary email address. After that you can try to login again.
